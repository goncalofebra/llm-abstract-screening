# RV4 criteria
# PubMed query, last 10 years, Free Full Text

Inclusion criteria:
1. Questionnaire, survey, or attitude study targeted at healthcare professionals (clinicians, nurses, residents, etc.)
2. Free full text available (open access or institutional access)
3. Written in English
4. Published in the last 10 years
5. Topic involves AI / machine learning / deep learning applied to clinical decision-making or clinical decision support systems (CDSS)

Exclusion criteria:
1. Does not match the inclusion criteria (eg. not a survey of clinicians, not about AI/ML CDSS)
2. Out of context (eg. AI applied to operations, billing, education, or non-clinical decision-making)
3. High risk of bias (eg. extremely small sample, no methods reported, unclear participant selection)
4. Full text not freely available
5. Not in English
6. Published more than 10 years ago
